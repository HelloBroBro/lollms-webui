New Video Release Feature

We're excited to introduce a new feature for video releases:

  • When new content is released, you'll see different icons based on the content type:
    • A play button for new videos
    • A microphone icon for new podcasts
    • A chalkboard icon for new tutorials
    • A music note icon for new music
  • After consuming the content, the icon won't appear again for that specific item.
  • This helps you easily identify new, unwatched or unlistened content in your feed.

"Never miss a new release and keep track of your watched content effortlessly!"
